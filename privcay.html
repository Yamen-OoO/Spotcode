Hosting

Our team partners with leading hosting providers worldwide to offer a range of hosting services and provide you with the best deals and offers to improve your business model and achieve future objectives.

Our offerings include dedicated server hosting, VPS hosting for businesses, and other services.

Our dedicated server team works closely with the support team of your hosting provider to maintain and secure your applications and websites.

Support

Our support services are divided into free and paid support services, available when purchasing hosting services:

  1. Free Support: Lifetime support with a dedicated agent for clear communication and fast problem resolution via email. Response time during peak hours is usually 4-6 hours. We provide assistance with resolving server issues, setting up the appropriate configuration, and providing links to optimize your server. The assistance is available round the clock.
  2. Paid Support: pricing ranges from $300 for 3 months to $1,100 for 12 months Free hosting support is included with the purchase of any hosting and managed services package.
