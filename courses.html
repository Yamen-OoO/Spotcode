Spotcode is a growing tech startup in Syria, is made up of a talented team of programmers and designers aged between 20 and 26. We are excited to share that our company is achieving great success in developing high-quality software for specific businesses.

To help more people become programmers and potentially join our team, Spotcode is launching new training courses. These courses will provide hands-on learning opportunities for individuals interested in expanding their programming skills with us.

The courses offered cover a wide range of topics, starting from the basics of programming to web development encompassing both frontend and backend aspects. Participants will have the opportunity to explore popular programming languages such as JavaScript and Python, which are highly sought after in the industry.

Additionally, the curriculum includes a module on mobile development using Flutter, a versatile framework for creating applications that work on multiple platforms. Participants will also engage in a specialized course on artificial intelligence, gaining insights into the latest technologies and applications in this rapidly evolving field.
